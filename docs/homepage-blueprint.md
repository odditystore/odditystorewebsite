# Oddity Store Homepage Blueprint

## 1. Navigation Recommendation

Recommended top navigation:

- Keycaps
- Keyboards
- Desk Mats
- Cups
- Cases
- About

The logo should serve as the `Home` entry.

Utility actions on the right side:

- Search
- Account / Login
- Cart

This is cleaner than keeping one large `Products` navigation item plus many extra links.

### Category-first browsing logic

- Keycaps
- Keyboards
- Desk Mats
- Cups
- Cases

Category rules:

- `Keycaps / 键帽` should include keycap sets and keycap protective films
- `Keyboards / 键盘` should include your Great Writer keyboard line, keyboard kits, and laptop keyboard protectors
- `Cases / 壳类` should include phone cases and earphone cases

Support destinations should live outside the primary navigation:

- `Shop All / 全部商品`
- `Collections / 系列`
- `New Releases / 新品发售`
- `Shipping & FAQ / 配送与常见问题`

These can appear in homepage sections, footer links, search overlays, or secondary page navigation.

## 2. Homepage Section Order

Recommended order:

1. Hero Carousel
2. New Releases
3. Shop by Category
4. Shop by Collection
5. Featured Products
6. Reviews & Testimonials
7. Creator / Video Showcase
8. What We Offer
9. Brand Story
10. Social Media
11. Global Shopping Info
12. Footer

## 3. Section Goals

### 1. Hero Carousel

Purpose:

- make products the first impression
- use strong large-format images
- introduce a collection or flagship product in each slide

Recommended CTA pair:

- Shop Now
- View Collection

### 2. New Releases

Purpose:

- quickly tell users there are many real products
- create freshness
- push conversion early

Recommended quantity:

- 4 to 8 cards

### 3. Shop by Category

Purpose:

- help users browse all goods faster
- support full-catalog structure

Recommended categories:

- Keycaps
- Keyboards
- Desk Mats
- Cups
- Cases

### 4. Shop by Collection

Purpose:

- present your product worlds, not just merchandise types

Recommended collections:

- Literary Archives
- Arcane Studies
- Entry Points
- Great Writer Boards
- Desk Mats
- Cup Series
- Cases

### 5. Featured Products

Purpose:

- highlight the strongest products regardless of category
- show best design taste, not only newest items

V1 should explicitly include:

- at least one `Great Writer / 大文豪` keyboard product
- at least one `Cup / 杯子` product

### 6. Reviews & Testimonials

Purpose:

- increase trust
- prove satisfaction
- give social proof for overseas buyers

Recommended content:

- 3 to 6 customer quotes
- star or satisfaction indicators if available
- optional buyer photos

### 7. Creator / Video Showcase

Purpose:

- show product exposure from bloggers or your own channels
- make the site feel active and visible

Recommended content:

- embedded or linked video cards
- creator thumbnails
- short quote or one-line caption

Suggested labels:

- Seen on creators
- In motion
- From the studio

### 8. What We Offer

Purpose:

- explain your studio value
- add confidence similar to stronger commerce sites

Recommended service/value cards:

- Designed by our studio
- Themed collectible collections
- Worldwide shipping
- Careful release support
- Responsive communication
- Premium packaging

### 9. Brand Story

Purpose:

- support the product world
- explain the visual and conceptual identity of Oddity Store

This should stay concise on the homepage.

### 10. Social Media

Purpose:

- bring visitors into your long-term audience
- create repeat traffic around launches

Recommended items:

- Instagram
- YouTube
- TikTok
- any other active channels

### 11. Global Shopping Info

Purpose:

- reduce friction before purchase

Recommended cards:

- shipping regions
- order type: in stock / pre-order / limited release
- support response time
- payment confidence

## 4. Recommendation For V1 Visual Emphasis

If the homepage has limited screen space, these sections should receive the most visual weight:

- Hero Carousel
- New Releases
- Shop by Collection
- Reviews & Testimonials
- Creator / Video Showcase

These sections will make the site feel both premium and alive.

## 5. Content We Will Need Later

To build this properly, we will later need:

- hero product images
- collection cover images
- product card images
- customer review quotes or screenshots
- blogger / creator links
- your official social media account links
- a short list of the services or promises you want to present
- category mapping for phone cases, earphone cases, and keycap protective films
