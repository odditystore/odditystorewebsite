Frequently Asked Questions

How do I switch between Bluetooth devices?
Hold FN and press Q, W, or E to switch between Bluetooth channels 1, 2, and 3. To pair a new device on a channel, hold the FN + channel key for 3 seconds until the LED blinks rapidly, then connect from your device's Bluetooth settings.
How do I check the battery level?
Press FN + Space. The RGB LED will briefly flash to indicate battery status: green (above 60%), yellow (20–60%), or red (below 20%).
How do I switch between Windows and Mac mode?
Press FN + M to toggle between Windows and Mac key mapping. In Mac mode, the Win key becomes Command and Alt becomes Option. The keyboard will briefly flash to confirm the switch.
My keyboard isn't connecting. What should I do?
First, try holding FN + Left Shift + Backspace for 3 seconds to reset the keyboard. Then re-pair your device from scratch. Make sure the keyboard is not already paired to another device on the same channel.
Are Oddity Store keycaps compatible with my keyboard?
All Oddity Store keycap sets use MX-compatible stems and are designed to work with both Great Writer 75 and Great Writer 100 keyboard kits. Check individual keycap product pages for specific layout compatibility.
How do I turn RGB off to save battery?
Press FN + Enter to toggle RGB lighting on or off. Disabling RGB significantly extends battery life in wireless mode.
