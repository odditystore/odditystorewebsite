# Oddity Store Official Site Brief

## 1. Project Goal

Build an overseas-facing official website for `不现实商店 / Oddity Store`.

The site should do two things at the same time:

- introduce the brand and its aesthetic world
- showcase and sell products internationally

This is not a pure marketplace clone of Taobao, but it also should not be only a brand showcase.
The recommended direction is a `product-led official site with strong brand framing`.

## 2. Recommended Positioning

### Core Direction

`Oddity Store` should be presented as a narrative design brand that turns literature, archives, mystery, fantasy, and found-document aesthetics into collectible desktop objects.

### Why this direction fits

- the current product lines already have strong themes, not generic SKUs
- the visual references point to dossiers, reports, identity files, stamps, and archival paper
- overseas buyers usually respond better to a clear brand world than to a crowded store homepage

### Working Brand Statement

Oddity Store creates collectible desk objects inspired by stories, documents, symbols, and imagined worlds.

## 3. Audience

Primary audience:

- mechanical keyboard enthusiasts
- collectors of themed desk setups
- buyers who like limited and story-rich products
- overseas users who value design language over low-price volume shopping

Secondary audience:

- fans of literary, mystery, fantasy, and cinematic aesthetics
- gift buyers

## 4. Recommended Website Type

For V1, the site should be:

- product first
- brand supported
- able to carry the full catalog
- English first, with Chinese support later if needed

This means:

- homepage focuses on product-driven storytelling
- the site architecture should support all current and future products
- homepage still highlights featured launches and core collections first
- deeper collection and shop pages can hold the complete catalog

## 5. Visual Direction

### Mood

- classified dossier
- archival paper
- surveillance report
- redaction marks
- typewriter and editorial serif contrast
- museum shop meets intelligence file

### Important balance

The site should feel dramatic and collectible, but the shopping layer must still feel trustworthy and clean.

So the design should combine:

- expressive art direction in hero carousel and collection sections
- clear product cards, price, shipping, filters, and checkout paths

## 7. Naming Strategy For Overseas

This matters a lot.

For overseas presentation, the safest and strongest approach is:

- keep `Oddity Store` as the master brand
- translate collection names in a polished editorial way
- revise or soften directly risky franchise wording where IP exposure is obvious

Suggested collection naming style:

- Literary Archives
- Arcane Studies
- Entry Points
- Cup Series
- Great Writer Boards

Product detail pages can preserve more personality, but public-facing collection names, hero copy, and navigation should be reviewed case by case for risk.

The practical rule should be:

- keep the product spirit
- keep the design identity
- adjust names and copy when needed to reduce obvious infringement exposure overseas

## 9. Homepage Structure

### Hero Carousel

Goal:

- lead with large rotating product visuals
- let products define the first impression of the brand
- include short product-led copy with supporting brand language
- give two obvious actions: shop now / explore collections

The hero should not feel like a corporate brand banner.
It should feel like a launch carousel for a collectible design studio.

### Featured Collections

Recommended modules:

- Literary Archives
- Arcane Studies
- Great Writer Boards
- Desk Mats

These modules should link into deeper collection pages that can eventually contain the full product range.

### Featured Products

Show the strongest products first on the homepage, while keeping access to all products elsewhere on the site.
Recommended first batch for homepage:

- 4 to 6 keycap sets
- 1 Great Writer keyboard
- 1 cup product
- 1 to 2 desk mats or accessories

But the full site structure should support:

- all active products
- upcoming products
- archived or sold out products if desired later

### Brand Method

Explain:

- inspiration
- material and production choices
- packaging and collectible intent

This section should support the products, not overpower them.

### Global Shopping Info

Include:

- international shipping
- release model or stock model
- payment
- support / FAQ

## 10. V1 Strategy Recommendation

Recommended launch order:

1. Build a strong homepage
2. Build `Shop All` and collection pages that can hold the full catalog
3. Build one product detail template
4. Add shipping / FAQ
5. Expand product data and archive structure gradually

This is better than trying to build every commerce edge case at once, while still keeping room for all products from the beginning.

## 11. Risks And Notes

- Some themes may have IP sensitivity for overseas markets
- Product naming and copy should be reviewed before public launch
- Some products may need renamed collections, alternate titles, or safer descriptive copy
- A high-style homepage without shipping clarity will hurt conversion
- A crowded Taobao-style homepage will weaken the brand image
- The right solution is not fewer products, but better product hierarchy
