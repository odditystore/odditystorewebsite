Welcome to the Great Writer 100. This manual covers the physical mode switch, Bluetooth pairing, key functions, backlight customization, and battery use. Keep this document for future reference.

02

Physical Mode Switch

The Great Writer 100 features a three-position physical switch on the side of the keyboard. Slide to the appropriate position before connecting.

B
Bluetooth
Wireless connection via Bluetooth 3.0 or 5.0. Supports up to 3 paired devices.
G
2.4G Wireless
Connect via the included USB dongle. Plug in the receiver and slide to G.
U
USB Wired
Connect with USB-C cable. Also charges the battery when in U mode.
Charging

Set switch to U, insert USB-C cable. During charging, the FN key LED turns red and syncs with the backlight. Input voltage must be 5V / 1A. Do not expose to direct sunlight — overvoltage may damage the product and void warranty.

03

Connection Guide

WIN Mode
Windows System
FNA
Press FN+A to activate Windows mode. Standard Windows key mapping applies.
MAC Mode
macOS / iOS
FNS
Press FN+S to activate Mac mode. Win key ↔ Command, Alt ↔ Option. F1–F12 work directly without FN.

Bluetooth pairing — with switch in B position:

Q
Bluetooth Device 1
Hold FN+Q until LED flashes rapidly. On your device, search for GW-100 BT3.0 or GW-100 BT5.0 and connect. LED stops flashing when paired.
W
Bluetooth Device 2
Hold FN+W until LED flashes rapidly. Search and connect from your second device.
E
Bluetooth Device 3
Hold FN+E until LED flashes rapidly. Search and connect from your third device.

Switching devices: In B mode, short-press FN+Q/W/E to switch between already-paired devices. Long-press to re-enter pairing mode for that channel.

2.4G: Slide switch to G, plug USB receiver into PC, and the keyboard connects automatically — no additional setup needed.

Wired: Slide switch to U, connect USB-C cable between keyboard and PC. Keyboard functions normally while charging.

07

Custom RGB Recording

The Great Writer 100 supports per-key custom lighting. You can record your own backlight pattern and save it to the keyboard's memory.

Recording Key

FN + Home = Enter / exit custom backlight recording mode

1
Enter Custom Mode
Press FN + 1, FN + 2, FN + @, or FN + 3# to enter one of the custom lighting slots. All LEDs will turn on to indicate you are in custom mode.
2
Start Recording
Press FN + Home to enter recording mode. All backlight LEDs will light up fully.
3
Assign Keys & Colors
Select the keys you want to light up. Choose your preferred colors for each key using the color cycle function.
4
Save & Exit
Press FN + Home again to exit recording mode and save your custom pattern. The keyboard stores the layout in memory and it persists after power cycling.

Note: Custom RGB recording is only available on the full-RGB version of Great Writer 100. Single-color backlight models do not support custom recording.

08

Battery & Charging

Battery 4000 mAh
Input 5V / 1A via USB-C. Do not exceed rated voltage — overvoltage voids warranty.
Charging Set switch to U, connect USB-C cable. FN key LED turns red and syncs with backlight during charging.
Caution Do not leave the keyboard in direct sunlight. Store at room temperature. Avoid prolonged exposure to heat sources.
Factory Reset Hold FN + Del for 3 seconds. All Bluetooth pairing data and custom settings will be cleared.
